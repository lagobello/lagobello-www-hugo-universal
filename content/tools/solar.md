## Solar calculator FAQ

### Can I use this solar calculator for a Lago Bello lot in Brownsville, Texas?

Yes. Set the location near Brownsville and adjust the panel count, panel area, efficiency, and electricity price to model a future Lago Bello home or lot.

### Does the calculator estimate exact savings or installation cost?

No. It estimates solar energy generation and the possible electricity value of that production. Final savings depend on home design, roof orientation, shade, utility rate plans, installer pricing, incentives, permitting, and interconnection requirements.

### Why plan solar before building a home?

Planning early helps you reserve suitable roof area, choose orientation, estimate electrical demand, and compare lot and home designs before construction decisions become expensive to change.
