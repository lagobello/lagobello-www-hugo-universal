# Builder Specifications

Detailed technical data for construction and development at Lago Bello.

## Infrastructure & Utilities

*   **Electrical:** Fully buried electrical design (Brownsville PUB).
*   **Water/Sewer:** Municipal water and waste water infrastructure installed.
*   **Drainage:** Storm drainage infrastructure and retention wall on Lake boundary complete.
*   **Streets:** 28-foot wide paved streets.

## Lot Specifications

*   **Zoning:** Residential Single Family.
*   **Setbacks:** Standard city setbacks apply (refer to plat map).
*   **Soil Conditions:** Geotechnical reports available upon request.

## Construction Guidelines

*   **Architectural Control:** All plans must be approved by the Architectural Control Committee (ACC).
*   **Minimum Square Footage:**
    *   Homes adjoining the Common Area Lake must contain a minimum of 2,000 sq ft of living area.
    *   All other homes must contain a minimum of 1,800 sq ft of living area.
