# Walk to School Convenience

Say goodbye to long carpool lines and stressful morning commutes. At
Lago Bello, your children can safely walk to school.

## The "No Car Line" Lifestyle

Lago Bello is the closest residential subdivision to South Texas
Independent School District's **Medical Professions** campus
(commonly known locally as the South Texas Academy for Medical
Professionals). For families whose kids attend that school, the daily
commute is a sidewalk.

* **Save Time.** No more waiting in hour-long drop-off or pick-up
  lines.
* **Healthy Living.** A short daily walk is exercise that doesn't feel
  like exercise.
* **Community.** Parents bump into each other on the walking paths;
  kids walk together. It works the way it used to.
* **Independence.** A short, safe walk teaches kids early how to
  manage time, weather, and getting where they need to be.

## A Magnet School Worth the Walk

The Medical Professions campus is a STISD magnet program — admission
is by application and lottery, drawing students from across the Rio
Grande Valley. The curriculum is geared toward students considering
careers in medicine, nursing, and allied health, with dual-credit
coursework, lab facilities, and pathways into Texas Southmost College
and UTRGV partnerships.

For families with a kid already attending — or hoping to — Lago Bello
turns the daily logistics from a regional commute into a five-minute
walk.

## Safe Walking Paths

The community is designed with kids on foot in mind:

* **Sidewalks.** Wide, well-maintained sidewalks throughout the
  neighborhood.
* **Crosswalks.** ADA-compliant pedestrian crossings.
* **Traffic Control.** 28-foot streets with calming geometry that
  keeps through-traffic speeds low. Because Lago Bello is gated and
  the streets are private, there's no cut-through traffic at all —
  just neighbors driving home.
* **Lighting.** Street and sidewalk lighting along the routes used
  most for school walks.

## Other Schools and Childcare Nearby

Beyond STISD Medical Professions, families typically choose between:

* **Los Fresnos CISD** elementary and middle schools to the north.
* **Brownsville ISD** options to the south, including the magnet
  programs at BISD's Pace and Hanna campuses.
* **Texas Southmost College** and **UTRGV** for college-aged students,
  both within a 20-minute drive.
* Several private and parochial options in central Brownsville.
