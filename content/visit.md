## Location

**Lago Bello Subdivision**
1000 Vittorio Alfieri Ave.
Olmito, Texas 78575

Lago Bello sits just north of Brownsville along I-69E, adjacent to South
Texas Independent School District's Medical Professions campus. You'll
recognize the entrance by the fountain just inside the front gate.

## When to Visit

- **Mornings (8–11 AM)** are good for walking the streets and seeing
  active construction. The Caminata trail catches the best light.
- **Late afternoon (5–7 PM)** shows off the lake and the western
  views. Bring a phone — the photos sell the lots better than we can.
- **Weekends** are quietest. Weekdays may have crews on site.

## What to Expect at the Gate

Lago Bello is a private gated subdivision. For a self-guided drive-
through, the gate is open during normal daylight hours; you can drive
the loop, see the lake, and leave at your own pace. Please respect that
homes here are already occupied — no entering yards or knocking on
doors.

If you'd like an actual walkthrough of available lots — including ones
that aren't visible from the street — schedule a guided tour first
(below). We'll meet you at the gate.

## Schedule a Guided Tour

The most useful visit is a guided one with someone who can answer
questions about lot pricing, HOA, building constraints, and what's
under construction nearby. Most tours run 30–60 minutes.

[Contact us](/contact/) to schedule, or call/message any of the
[listing agents](/lots/) directly — agents on listed lots can usually
arrange a same-day visit.
