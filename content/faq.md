> In case you don't find the answer for your questions please feel free to contact us, we will be happy to help you.

## 1. WHAT IS THE STATUS OF THE PROJECT

Lago Bello Section 1 and Section 2 are complete. Section 2 lots are available for sale.
Section 3 is currently in the Design phase.

## 2. WHAT INFRASTRUCTURE IS BEING DEVELOPED AT LAGO BELLO

The following infrastructure has been completed for Lago Bello Subdivision

* Fully buried electrical design in collaboration with Brownsville PUB
* High flow fire-fighting installation
* Water and Waste Water infrastructure
* Storm Drainage Infrastructure
* Retention wall on the Lake boundary
* 28 foot wide street
* Private Gate for Residents
* Fountain at the Entrance of Lago Bello

## 3. SE HABLA ESPAÑOL?

¡Sí! Como la mayor parte de la zona Rio Grande al sur de Texas, hablamos español y estamos prontos a recibir sus pedidos por informes.

## 4. HOW MUCH DO LOTS COST AT LAGO BELLO?

Lot prices range from $75,000 to $175,000 depending on size, location, and proximity to the lake. Waterfront lots command a premium. Visit our [lots page](/lots/) for current pricing and availability.

## 5. WHAT SIZE ARE THE LOTS?

Lot sizes range from approximately 7,000 to 16,000 square feet. Lakefront lots tend to be larger. See our [interactive map](/interactive-map/) for detailed lot dimensions.

## 6. IS FINANCING AVAILABLE?

Yes. We work with preferred local lenders to offer lot loans, construction-to-permanent loans, and investment property loans. We also support foreign national and international borrower loans. See our [investment page](/investment/) for details.

## 7. CAN I CHOOSE MY OWN BUILDER?

Yes, you can bring your own builder. We also have a [builders page](/builders/) with specifications and technical requirements for construction at Lago Bello.

## 8. HOW CLOSE IS LAGO BELLO TO BROWNSVILLE?

Lago Bello is located in Olmito, just minutes north of Brownsville, TX. The subdivision is conveniently located near schools, shopping, and major roadways.

## 9. DOES LAGO BELLO HAVE HIGH-SPEED INTERNET?

Yes! All 30 residential lots are fiber-ready with [Omni Fiber](/blog/2025/12/10/omni-fiber/) high-speed internet. All infrastructure is 100% buried underground for reliability.

## 10. IS THE LAKE NATURAL OR MAN-MADE?

The 16-acre freshwater lake is man-made and is the only one of its kind in Brownsville. At its deepest point it will be around 20 feet deep. The lake is privately owned and maintained by the Home Owners Association. Learn more on our [waterfront page](/waterfront/).

## 11. ARE THERE HOA FEES?

Yes. Lago Bello has a Home Owners Association. HOA fees and details are available at [lagohoa.org](https://lagohoa.org).
