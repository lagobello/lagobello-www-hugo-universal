Community & Lifestyle

Walk to School

Lago Bello is the closest residential subdivision to South Texas Independent School District’s Medical Professions campus. Many lots are a short walk away — no carpool line.

Lake & Caminata

A 16-acre privately-owned freshwater lake sits at the heart of the community, with the Caminata trail circling it. Combustion-free watercraft only; lakefront homes have the right to a private dock.

Utilities & Infrastructure

  • Buried electrical (Brownsville PUB)
  • Omni Fiber pre-installed to the lot
  • Municipal water & wastewater
  • 28-foot paved streets, ADA sidewalks
  • FEMA Zone X / X (shaded) — no flood-insurance mandate

HOA & Gated Access

Lago Bello is a private gated subdivision — single entry, fenced perimeter, no through-traffic. Streets, lake, and Caminata are all owned by the lot owners through the HOA. See lagohoa.org for fees and rules.

Getting Around

Brownsville/SPI International Airport ~15 min  ·  Downtown Brownsville ~15 min  ·  Matamoros (B&M Bridge) ~20 min  ·  SpaceX Starbase ~35 min  ·  South Padre Island ~30 min  ·  McAllen ~50 min.
